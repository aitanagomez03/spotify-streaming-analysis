# spotify-streaming-analysis

🎧 Streaming Analysis of My Spotify Listening History  
This project is a data‑driven exploration of my personal Spotify streaming habits. Using Python and interactive visualizations, I analyze how much time I've spent over the last few years listening to music and when do I listen the most out of every hour, day and month.  
🌟 Project Overview  
The goal of this project is to transform raw Spotify data into meaningful insights. By combining descriptive analytics, audio‑feature exploration, and visual storytelling, the project highlights patterns in my listening activity, my top artists and tracks.   
🎯 Objectives  
- Explore my most‑played artists, tracks, and genres
- Visualize listening behavior across days, months, and hours
- Practice data cleaning, transformation, and visualization techniques
- Experiment with the Spotify Web API and personal streaming data  
🛠️ Technologies Used  
- Python
- Jupyter Notebook
- Power BI for dashboards  
📦 Data Sources  
The dataset comes from the source:
- Spotify Personal Data Export (“Your Data” package), which includes JSON files with detailed streaming history   
📊 Main Analyses  
- Total minutes streamed since 2015
- Top artists and tracks
- Listening frequency by hour of the day, day of the week and month  
📈 Visualizations  
The project includes a variety of visual outputs, such as:
- Bar charts with my top 10 most listened artists and top 5 most listened song, with the minutes listened
- Bar charts of time of day, day of the week and month where I listen most music  
🧠 Key Insights  
Some of the most interesting findings include:
- The times of day, day of the week and month when I listen to music the most
- My top artists and tracks
